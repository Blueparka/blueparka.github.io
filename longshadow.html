Product Overview

Create flat design long shadows with one click. Freely control color, length, angle, and opacity.

Long Shadow FX is a free Photoshop plugin that allows you to instantly generate beautiful long shadow effects on text and shape layers. Perfect for modern UI/UX design, logos, and icons.

Key Features:

• Customize shadow color, length, angle, and opacity
• Create and manage multiple long shadows on a single canvas
• Edit your text freely – the long shadow stays intact
• Preserves existing layer effects (glow, stroke, etc.) except drop shadow

Whether you're designing for web, mobile, or print, Longshadow streamlines your workflow and helps you achieve professional results in seconds.
