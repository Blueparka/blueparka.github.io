Product Overview

MOOSUN is a retro-style arcade shooter that runs directly inside the Photoshop panel.

Have you ever been working in Photoshop and thought, "I just need a quick break"? When you're stuck on an idea, or just need to reset your brain for a moment. "What if there was a game I could play right inside Photoshop?" — That thought is exactly what made MOOSUN happen.

And hey, if you've never really used Photoshop before, you're welcome here too. Just open the panel and play. We believe creativity and playfulness live in the same place. Sometimes a tiny distraction is exactly what sparks the next big idea. MOOSUN is here for those moments — a small piece of joy, tucked inside your workspace.

Features:

• 5 unique biomes: Volcanic Zone, Crystal Caves, Orbital Colony, Glacial Expanse, Energy Core
• Multiple enemy types with unique attack patterns
• Boss battles
• Pulse shot & Bomb that destroys ground structures
• Hi-score tracking across sessions
• Endless gameplay — how far can you go?
• Runs entirely inside the panel for Photoshop — no browser, no external app
• Free to play. No sign-in required.

Controls:

• Arrow keys: Move
• Space: Shoot
• X: Bomb (destroys ground structures)
• Shift + Space: Pause

Note:

MOOSUN does not interact with your Photoshop documents or files.
If you experience uncontrolled shooting or unresponsive controls at launch, please restart Photoshop. This is a known behavior and resolves after restart.
