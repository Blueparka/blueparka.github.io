SafeSnap Panel – Product Overview

Price: USD $12.00

SafeSnap Panel is a plugin for Photoshop that allows users to precisely align not only text layers but also image and shape layers to various safety margins on the canvas (90%, 93%, and 95%).

You can snap a single layer or multiple selected layers at once, making it especially useful for streamlining layout work in professional design environments.

SafeSnap helps ensure your designs are properly aligned for a variety of display or print standards. While similar results may be possible through manual guides or actions, SafeSnap offers a much more intuitive and responsive UI that speeds up your workflow.

Note: Colored backgrounds shown in screenshots and videos are added for visual clarity and are not included in the plugin.
